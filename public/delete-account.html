Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from the Gujarati App, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at: qweweb2019@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. In the email body, please include the Email ID you used to log in via Google.

Once we receive your email, we will permanently delete your account profile and any saved data (like Biodata or Resumes) within 7 business days.

Note: If you simply uninstall the app, your data is not automatically deleted from our servers. You must request deletion via email.
